Aaron Camacho

Data Scientist

I recently graduated with a Master’s in Data Analytics and have spent the past 10 years honing my skills in analytics and database management. I am keen to continue developing my career in this field. Software fluencies include but are not limited to Excel, MySQL, Oracle SQL, SAS, R, Python. Statistical significance, data mining, A/B testing, regression modeling, machine learning algorithms, neural networks, text mining, and data-driven optimization are my professional passions.

Compiling scripts that analyze data and solve problems is another one of my passions. As I work with them, digits, spreadsheets, and unstructured data are turned into stories. These stories become actionable solutions and strategies for businesses in this age of big data. I take pride in my ability to lead companies and individuals to successful outcomes and look forward to discussing how my skills can help you in this way.

On a personal level, I am detail-oriented, organized, and precise in my work. Clear and illuminating presentations are a result of my strong communication skills. I’m comfortable facing the numbers on my own and bring a positive vibe, optimism, and love for solving problems to every project. Years of hands-on experience in the retail, financial, and information technology sectors allow me to see the big picture and bring actionable results that effect positive change all the way from raw data to the end-user.
